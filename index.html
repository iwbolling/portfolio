About Me

Professional Profile

I'm a junior developer with a passion for computers, coding and solving logic problems. I started with QBasic and video game modding in my youth, worked a bit with VisualBasic and have since started using JavaScript to develop web apps and games.

Interests & Personality

I'm a pretty social person, and have no problem talking in front of a large crowd. My mind has a rich curiosity for all things, and I love the creative arts as much as I'm fascinated by the sciences. I tend to be an indoors person, but I do love seeing different kinds of wildlife. Some of my many hobbies include:

  • Creative Writing: I'm always crafting settings and characters to use in my fiction.
  • Gaming: I love playing computer games as well as developing them!
  • Language: I've studied German, some Latin and even a bit of Mandarin Chinese.
  • Music: a huge passion of mine; I have quite broad tastes (over 20 genres and counting!)
